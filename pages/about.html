Our story

Why we built this

Most people take a loan without fully understanding what they are signing up for. The EMI looks affordable, the tenure feels manageable, and the rate sounds reasonable. But the real cost of borrowing is almost never visible upfront.

KnowYourEMI was built to fix that. We wanted a place where anyone could enter their loan details and instantly see the full picture: total interest paid, exact EMI, how a part payment changes things, and whether a balance transfer actually saves money.

We are not a bank, a broker, or a financial advisor. We do not sell leads or recommend specific lenders. The tools are free because the goal is to inform, not to sell.

What we offer

The calculators

EMI Calculator

Calculate your monthly installment for personal, home, car, or credit card loans. See the full amortization schedule, interest breakdown, and download a PDF summary.

Loan Eligibility Calculator

Estimate the loan amount you can qualify for based on your income, existing obligations, and tenure preference.

Balance Transfer Calculator

Compare your existing loan against a new lender offer. See exact foreclosure costs, new EMI, and whether switching actually saves you money after all fees.

Floating Rate Home Loan Simulator

Simulate how future rate changes affect your EMI and total interest. Useful when rates are moving and you want to plan ahead.

Credit Card Payoff Calculator

See how long it takes to clear your credit card balance, how much interest piles up, and what happens when you pay more than the minimum.
